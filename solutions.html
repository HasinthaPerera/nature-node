Solutions

What can be done to prevent or reduce the pollution that is happening in the world?

Here are some solutions that can be used to reduce air pollution.

  • Promote walking and cycling as alternative modes of transportation.
  • Promote the use of cleaner fuel options such as electric or hybrid vehicles.
  • Encourage the use of clean energy sources like solar or wind power for residential energy needs.
  • Regulate open burning (e.g., burning yard waste) and educate residents on its impact on air quality. and also provide alternatives such as composting or municipal waste collection.
  • Plant trees and shrubs in the town to act as natural air purifiers and provide shade.
  • Encourage community involvement in local environmental initiatives and clean-up efforts.
